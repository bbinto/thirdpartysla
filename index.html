3rd party SLA

3rd party SLA is a tool to score scripts that 3rd parties (people or companies outside your dev team) provide for you to put on your page. These scripts can save you a lot of time and provide neat features on your site, but they can also be security risks, cause performance problems, and make debugging a nightmare.

Call it the 'YSlow for widgets' if you will.
